About me

Eugenio Roverato

junior game designer

When I was a child I dreamed of being an inventor, I made plans for everything you can imagine and beyond.
Growing up I decided to put my creativity to the realization of a passion: game development.
While learning design, I found a goal to pursue: innovation, shaping the past towards the demands of the present.
Now I like to test myself with few resources and time to create enjoyable experiences.

I have experimented and failed a lot of projects, but this has allowed me to improve my prototyping skills .
I take inspiration from everything around me because my passion for design extends to different areas (interior design, UI/UX, product design).
